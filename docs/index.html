How It Works

⚽ The Model

Each team gets an attack strength and defense weakness score, blended from three signals:

  • Match history (2022+) — recent international form
  • Squad ratings — EA FC player quality for all 48 teams
  • Market odds — Bet365 correct-score implied power

Those feed into independent Poisson distributions. Validated at ±0.236 goals MAE vs Bet365.

📊 Expected Goals (xG)

xG is the average goals a team is expected to score — not a guarantee. An xG of 2.0 means scoring exactly 2 is only ~27% likely; 1–1 can beat 2–1 because both teams need specific goal counts.

Model odds = 1 ÷ probability, no bookmaker margin. Odds of 2.50 ≈ 40% model chance before margin.

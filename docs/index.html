What do these numbers mean?

Expected Goals (xG) — the average goals a team is expected to score in this matchup. An xG of 2.0 doesn't mean exactly 2 goals; it means that over many replays of this fixture they would average 2 per game.

Win / Draw % — the chance each team wins or the match ends level, derived from a Poisson score model. All three always sum to 100%.

Fair odds — simply 1 ÷ probability, with no bookmaker margin baked in. Odds of 2.50 mean a 40% model chance.

Model pick — the win/draw outcome with the highest probability. It can differ from the most likely exact scoreline: for xG ≈ 2, scoring exactly 1 or 2 goals are nearly equally likely (~27% each), which is why a 1–1 can edge out a 2–1.

The model blends international match history (2022+), EA FC squad ratings and betting market power into attack / defence strengths, feeding independent Poisson distributions (validated at ±0.236 goals MAE vs Bet365 correct-score odds).
